On August 20 of 2011, Marc ANDREESSEN published a blog post titled: ["Why software is eating the world"](https://a16z.com/why-software-is-eating-the-world/).

At that time, if you were a young beginner in I.T, you already knew that software was eating the world as you were already so eager about it that you literally dedicated every days, evening and weekend learning, developing, crafting and hacking prototypes of all kind.

I remember the excitment and energy of that era (2004/2014), everything was possible, the futur was insanely bright and promising.

Ten years fast forward since that periode of time, we had up and down, but, what we can all agree upon, is that Marc ANDREESSEN statement never was so true than today with A.I.

Some could argue that A.I is the new hype, the new cool kid in town and they would be right, it's the current hot topic, however, where they're wrong is it not going anywhere.

A.I is evolving, changing and reshaping the world at a pace that I never witness before.

I've lived the internet growth, the emergence of smartphones, build part of the cloud and been passionated with crypto, they all had their own growth rate and fast increase of interest and community adoption, but gosh, never was it so vivid that A.I.

The amount of interest, money and power we've been able to concentrate in recent years around this topic is insane.
The improvements and net gains in productivity are real even if company yet struggle to properly integrate it, they immediately and for once, correctly, feel it will be a game changer of the industrial revolution magnitude for humanity.

But lets be honest, as for every major breakthrough if we're not careful, there will be harsh consequences for real people.
